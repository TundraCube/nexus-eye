# Privacy Policy for Nexus-Eye

**Nexus-Eye** is committed to your privacy. This policy explains our handling of your information.

## 1. Data Collection
**Nexus-Eye does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.** 
- We do not have access to your GitHub account, your code, or your personal information.
- All code analysis and syntax highlighting are performed locally within your browser.

## 2. Data Usage
- The extension uses the `activeTab` permission strictly to apply styles to the current GitHub Pull Request page you are viewing.
- The `storage` permission is used locally on your machine to save your preference for whether the highlighting is enabled or disabled.

## 3. Third-Party Access
- No data is ever sent to third-party servers.
- We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
- We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page.
